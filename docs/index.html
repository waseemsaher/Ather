what you just saw

An entire AI engineering
team in one tool.

Most AI tools are a single brilliant, expensive generalist. Aether is a whole company — a CEO, managers, and specialists — and each one uses only as much AI as their job actually needs.

the idea

Don't hire one genius.
Hire a company.

A strategic mind at the top handles the hard calls. Managers coordinate. Specialists do the focused work. The colour tells you the cost.

The CEO

Opus-class · premium

The most capable, most expensive mind. Only fires for genuinely hard decisions — never the busywork.

The managers

Sonnet-class · balanced

Five department heads who break big jobs into pieces and hand them to the right specialist.

The specialists

Haiku-class · cheap & fast

Cheap, fast, and excellent at one focused thing. They quietly handle about 80% of the work.
